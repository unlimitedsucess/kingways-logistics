About Us

Ultimate company with Years Of Professionalism

kingsway Logistics is one of the worldwide top logistics company with a strong track record in E-commerce-tailored logistics which is powered by technology.

kingsway Logistics was launched in 2004 with the premise that logistics is necessary for both social and electronic commerce. We followed through on a straightforward objective: eliminate the usual problems related to last-mile delivery and simplify logistics for people and businesses anywhere, whenever.

More on Us

We also offer diplomats service

kingsway Logistics is considered to be the leading supplier to the transportation industry in America. kingsway Logistics has forged long term (wholesale) relationships / partnerships with all the major airlines, express courier integrators, worldwide freighter networks, charter airlines, trucking systems and logistics suppliers. Based on our significant shipping volumes, we are in a position to offer preferential pricing and superior service to you, our important and valued customer.
